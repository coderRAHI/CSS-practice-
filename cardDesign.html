Radha & Krishna......

             It was said that they fell in love during 
             childhood and spent their days together.Radha,
              like Krishna, was born to a mortal family, 
              though she had a divine nature.In some sects
             of Hinduism, these two deities are held to be 
             the supreme entities of the world. This branch 
             of the faith is called Krishnaism.
